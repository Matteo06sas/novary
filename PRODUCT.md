# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Ambitious Italian businesses, brands, and professionals — founders, entrepreneurs, small–mid businesses — who want a premium digital presence that communicates authority and builds trust on first impression. They have high standards and can perceive quality, but lack the time or skill to produce it themselves. They arrive at Novary looking for a partner, not a vendor.

## Product Purpose

Novary is a small Italian premium digital studio. The website's job is to generate trust fast and convert visits into consultancy requests. The site is Novary's strongest portfolio piece — if the site itself doesn't feel premium, the pitch collapses. Success = a visitor who feels "this is exactly the level I want" and books a call.

## Brand Personality

Preciso · Ambizioso · Essenziale

Voice is direct and confident, never boastful. Copy cuts fast. The brand believes good work speaks for itself, so it doesn't over-explain. Tone in Italian: authoritative but not cold, precise but not clinical.

## Anti-references

Generic agency templates (Webflow/ThemeForest aesthetic): busy layouts, gradient-heavy decorations, hero-metrics, forgettable iconography, everything shouting at once. If it looks like it was assembled from a template library, it's wrong.

## Design Principles

1. **Il sito è la prova** — The Novary site must be the most polished thing the visitor has seen that day. Every page, every section, every detail is a case study in itself. There is no "good enough."
2. **Essenzialità come forza** — Complexity removed, not hidden. Every element on screen must earn its place. What remains is what matters.
3. **Autorevolezza senza volume** — Confidence through restraint, not spectacle. No tricks, no excess motion, no decorative complexity. Presence through precision.
4. **Conversione come obiettivo silenzioso** — Every design choice points toward trust and contact. Beautiful, but never self-indulgent. The hierarchy always leads somewhere.
5. **Dettaglio invisibile, effetto visibile** — Users never notice the micro-decisions. They just feel that something is right. Precision compounds.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Good color contrast on all text. Full keyboard navigation. Reduced-motion support already partially in place (`prefers-reduced-motion` global reset) — extend it per-component where motion is purposeful rather than decorative. No known specific user needs beyond standard AA compliance.
